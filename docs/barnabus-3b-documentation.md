> "We are a volunteer-led initiative building a foundation for artificial intelligence rooted firmly in Biblical principles. ---

## Overview

**Barnabus-3B** is a specialized large language model trained on 2,003 carefully curated question-answer pairs covering biblical ethics, moral teachings, and scriptural principles. Named after Barnabas, the encourager and early church leader known for his generosity and faith (Acts 4:36-37), this model embodies the spirit of collaborative, purpose-driven innovation.

### Core Identity

- **Model Name**: Barnabus-3B
- **Training Data**: 2,003 ethics-focused Q&A pairs
- **Primary Domain**: Biblical ethics and moral reasoning
- **Output Philosophy**: Biblical truth, moral clarity, and neighborly love
- **Governance**: Volunteer-led initiative under KOKAI

---

## Purpose & Vision

### Why Barnabus-3B?

In an era of rapidly advancing artificial intelligence, there is a growing need for language models that reflect enduring moral foundations. Barnabus-3B was created to:

1. **Ground AI in Biblical Ethics**: Provide responses rooted in Scripture and Christian moral tradition.
2. **Promote Truth and Integrity**: Ensure outputs align with honesty, humility, and love for neighbor.
3. **Serve the Faith Community**: Equip believers, educators, and ministry leaders with an AI tool that understands biblical context.
4. **Model Faith-Driven Collaboration**: Demonstrate how volunteer-led, values-based initiatives can contribute meaningfully to the AI landscape.

### What Barnabus-3B Does

Barnabus-3B is designed to answer questions about:

- **Biblical Commands**: The Ten Commandments, greatest commandments, and summary teachings of Jesus
- **Moral Virtues**: Love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, self-control
- **Social Ethics**: Justice, mercy, humility, integrity, honesty, and care for the vulnerable
- **Christian Living**: Stewardship, work ethic, marriage ethics, sexual purity, and community responsibility
- **Scriptural Wisdom**: Teachings from every book of the Bible, from Genesis to Revelation

---

## Training Dataset

### Composition

The Barnabus-3B dataset consists of **2,003 instruction-response pairs** organized as follows:

- **200 Core Biblical Ethics Q&As**: Foundational questions with direct scriptural references
- **1 Boundary-Definition Pair**: Explicit guidelines for allowed and disallowed outputs
- **1,800 Generated Ethics Q&As**: Expanded coverage of biblical topics across all 66 books of the Bible

### Coverage Areas

The dataset spans the full biblical canon, with questions generated across:

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Old Testament Law** | Justice, honesty, stewardship, Jubilee, gleaning |
| **Wisdom Literature** | Proverbs on speech, Ecclesiastes on life's meaning, Job on integrity |
| **Prophets** | Isaiah on justice, Amos on righteousness, Micah on humility |
| **Gospels** | Beatitudes, parables, Jesus's ethical teachings |
| **Acts & Epistles** | Early church ethics, spiritual gifts, Christian freedom |
| **Boundary Safety** | Refusal protocols for harmful or unbiblical requests |

### Format

Each pair follows the standard instruction-response structure:

```json
{
  "instruction": "What does [Book] say about [Topic]?",
  "response": "[Scripture-backed answer with verse reference]"
}
```

## Ethical Boundaries

Barnabus-3B operates within clear biblical guardrails:

### Allowed Outputs

- Biblical truth and doctrinal explanation
- Ethical instruction rooted in Scripture
- Encouragement and moral support
- Historical and contextual biblical information

### Disallowed Outputs

- Content promoting hatred, malice, or division
- Glorification of sin or unethical behavior
- Sexually explicit or violent content
- Unnecessary, repetitive, or unsolicited preaching

When faced with requests outside these boundaries, Barnabus-3B responds by declining and redirecting toward biblically consistent topics (Matthew 5:28; 1 Thessalonians 4:3-5).

## Technical Foundation

### Model Architecture

- **Base**: Fine-tuned on foundation language model
- **Parameters**: 3B (3 billion)
- **Specialization**: Biblical ethics and moral reasoning
- **Context Window**: Optimized for dialogue and Q&A

### Training Methodology

1. **Curated Seed Data**: 200 handcrafted Q&As with verified scriptural references
2. **Boundary Definition**: Explicit safety and scope guidelines embedded in training
3. **Synthetic Expansion**: 1,800 additional pairs generated to broaden coverage
4. **Validation**: All responses checked against biblical text and theological consistency
